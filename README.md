# AllTheThings
This repo is the archive for things previously rumbling around in the collective brain of the [We Are Open co-op](http://weareopen.coop).

**Hello!** Our Github exists to document all the things going on inside our co-op that need recording. You'll find evolving resources, archives and more.

[We Are Open co-op](http://weareopen.coop) is a member-owned business that provides consultancy services. We exist to spread the culture, processes, and benefits of open wherever we can. We work everywhere that has an internet connection, with clients ranging from international development banks and global NGOs, to smaller organisations. 

We are a [company](https://beta.companieshouse.gov.uk/company/10150608) limited by guarantee without share capital registered in the UK. We Are Open have incorporated the [seven principles of international co-operation](https://github.com/weareopen/AllTheThings/wiki/The-Seven-Principles) in our [articles of incorporation](https://github.com/weareopen/AllTheThings/wiki/AllTheThings/assets/docs/wao-articles-of-incorporation.pdf) and are affiliated with [Co-operative Technologists](https://wiki.coops.tech).
